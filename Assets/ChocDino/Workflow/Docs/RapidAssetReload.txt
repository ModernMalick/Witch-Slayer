RAPID ASSET RELOAD Quick Start:

1) Open the Rapid Asset Reload window by going to the menu `Window > General > Chocolate Dinosaur > Rapid Asset Reload`.
2) Drag the window and dock it somewhere in your UI.
3) That's it!
